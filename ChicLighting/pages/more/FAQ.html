1. What if I purchase an item and then I see it somewhere else cheaper?

Not to worry! Chic Lighting has a Low-Price Guarantee so there is no reason to hesitate or wait to make your purchase! If you ever find the exact same product advertised for less anywhere, within 30 days of your Chic Lighting purchase, please let us know and we will happily match that price. Keep in mind that shipping and handling fees, as well as any applicable sales tax, will be taken into consideration.

2. Can I change my order?

Once we receive your order we get to work on it right away. This means that changes or cancellations are not always possible. You should also know that once an order is placed, it cannot be changed online. With all of this in mind, we still do understand that sometimes you want to ask us to try, so you may contact Customer Service to make a request for a change or cancellation. We will make our best efforts to apply the requested change.

3. How do I return a product?

If you want to return an item you may do so within 30 days of receipt as long as it is defined as a returnable item. Just contact Customer Service and we will provide pre-paid return labels. We will need your help in packaging up the item, adding the label to the box and making it available for pick up.

4. What do I do if my product has a problem during the warranty period?

If you experience a problem with any product you purchased from Chic Lighting that offers a manufacturer warranty, please contact Chic Lighting Customer Service. We will either facilitate a resolution or put you in contact with the manufacturer so that they might assist you directly.

5. What should I do if my credit card has been refused when placing an order?

Check that all the details you have entered on the checkout page are correct. If it is refused after that, try another credit card. Contact Customer Service if you feel that your card is being refused due to our error. In some cases we may recommend that you contact your credit card company to get the issue resolved.
